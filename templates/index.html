What gets masked

Names, emails, phones, government IDs, passports, bank details, credentials, addresses, DOB, and other strong PII patterns. Business codes like employee code, job code, cost center, and department references are preserved unless they clearly match a PII pattern.

How colleagues use it

They open the site, upload the file, enter a password or key if needed, and download the output. No local Python, no command line, no manual decryption steps.
